How I Started?

  It all changed when lockdown was announced. At starting, everything seemed to have come to a STANDSTILL... but, one day, I noticed some eye-cacthing birds from my balcony - They aroused my curiosity. Next morning, we were ready with the camera. Initialy, my father helped me but soon I got grip on camera
